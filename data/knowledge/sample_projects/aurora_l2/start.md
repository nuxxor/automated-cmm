# Aurora L2 Quickstart

The safest way to get started is to use the official docs, ask questions in public, and avoid unsolicited direct messages.

Support is handled in public support threads. Moderators will never DM first to ask for passwords, wallet keys, seed phrases, or remote access.

If a bridge deposit is delayed, check the status page first. Normal bridge transfers can take 10 to 30 minutes depending on the destination chain and the relayer queue.
